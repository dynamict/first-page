Best Buy Canada

E-Commerce Merchandising Specialist


I spent my time at Best Buy Canada as an E-Commerce Merchandising Specialist (EMS) for the Smart Home + Fitness/Sports & Recreation categories. Through these categories, I was able to apply different strategies to optimize different pages for higher conversion/awareness.

An EMS’s responsibility is to drive conversion/awareness once a user lands on the Best Buy website. By analyzing data and working with stakeholders, we create a customer journey that supports the user’s buying process.


Health & Wellness Campaign


Health & Wellness is an event at Best Buy Canada that takes place in January every year, promoting fitness and health equipment in line with New Year's resolutions.

Problems:

1. In past campaigns, the focus was to promote brands and items rather than the customer journey. Customers were presented with sales and products rather than options to explore their needs and interests.

2. Customers would click on different links from the landing page and have no way of returning to campaign pages, leading to high exit rates from subsequent pages.

3. The landing page bounce rate was abnormally high for what was expected from previous campaign landing pages.


Key Contributions:

• By analyzing data from the past campaigns, I was able to modify the customer journey to include more exploration rather than only presenting customers with sales.

• Using a CMS (ContentStack), I organized webpages to meet the needs of stakeholders to fit the requirements from sponsors while still maintaining exploration options for customers.

• Through re-branding the campaign to "Health & Wellness", it allowed more products to be featured on the page while still being in line with the theme.






Fitness & Sports Pages


Fitness & Sports represent a category at Best Buy Canada. Although Fitness & Sports may not be the first thing that comes to mind when shopping at Best Buy, it is one of the fastest growing categories in the company.

Problems:

1. Best Buy's fitness category felt out of place and did not connect well with the other products in the assortment, such as smartwatches and fitness trackers.

2. Information regarding products and ecosystems for fitness were non-existent. Customers did not know how products worked what assortment was present at Best Buy.

3. Landing page felt simple and uninformative, causing it to have a significantly higher bounce rate compared to similar pages on the Best Buy website.

Key Contributions:

• Cross-merchandized other categories within Best Buy to entice customers to continue shopping or to learn more about different products.

• Included additional widgets for customers to understand which products were "top sellers" of the week to make more informed decisions for themselves.

• Created blogs and articles so customers are able to research about Best Buy's products and make better buying decisions.

• Click here to learn about other pages that were changed in a similar way!

Impact: By re-furnishing and changing the narrative on the Fitness & Sports landing page, the category saw a 200% increase in sales YoY for the week of Black Friday, the highest dollar value in sales in the category's history.
